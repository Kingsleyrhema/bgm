BELIEVERS GLORIOUS MINISTRIES

BGM General Overseer

Uche Emmanuel Ndah, the servant of God anointed to carry out the divine mandate of drawing men to God for them to live a fulfilled life.

He is the president of God’s commission, Believers Glorious Prayer Ministry (BGPM), the mercy, solution, and praise land, located at No. 30 Sijuade Street by Odo Olowu, Ijesha Express, Surulere Lagos Nigeria. A church that has many branches presently in Nigeria, and with many followers from round the world. He is hails from Asaba, Delta State of Nigeria, married with five kids, three boys and two girls, and blessed all round with the goodness of God.

Wife of BGM General Overseer

Pst. Mrs. Onyinye Ifeyinwa Uche-Ndah, is the wife of the presiding pastor of Believers Glorious Prayer Ministry, Pst. Uche E. Ndah. She is a woman of great passion for the manifestation of God in the women that love God.

Mommy Glorious, as she is fondly called, runs the women fellowship group named Glorious Women, through which a lot of women receive divine transformation spiritually and in marriage. It is through this fellowship group that she also empowers women in their various carriers. She has a lot of ministry visions that she believes to attain through the help of God.

About BGM

Believers Glorious Prayer Ministry started as just a prayer meeting, with the name, Glorious Assembly, at No.30 Shonde Street, Ijesha Lagos in November 2009. In the year 2010, God gave the church a land in which it is still standing today, at No.30 Sijuade Street, Ijesha Lagos. In October 2012, the church was registered under the Corporate Affairs Commission of the Federal Republic of Nigeria (CAC) with the name “Believers Glorious Prayer Ministry”, by which it continued her prayer ministry until June 2015, when it started running her activities as a church by the direction of God. In February 2016, BGPM had her first branch at No.8 Ishen-Olu Estate Odogunyen, Ikorodu Lagos. Also in the year 2019, she had another branch in Oba, Anambra State, at Oba Old Road, by Ageep B/Stop Ayino’s compound, Isu Umunnakwa. And still planting more branches both nationwide and internationally. BGPM has raised a lot of destinies around the world, which has made her one of the fast-growing churches in Nigeria at this present time. We believe that the God that laid this foundation shall perfect His will over BGPM.
